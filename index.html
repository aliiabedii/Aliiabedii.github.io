About Me

I got my master’s degree in Civil Engineering from the Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) , where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Ghodrati Amiri. My research focused on structural Health Monitoring (SHM) of civil infrastructure, aiming to model structural behavior and improve the reliability of monitoring systems.

Research Interests

  • Signal & image processing
  • Vision-based inspection
  • Optimization of structures
  • Deep/Machine Learning
